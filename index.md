# MyStateTracker

Track which U.S. states you've visited and how fully you've explored them.

---

## Privacy Policy

MyStateTracker does not collect, sell, or share personal data.

Visit data is stored locally on your device.  
If iCloud sync is enabled, data is securely stored in your private iCloud account using Apple CloudKit. The developer does not have access to this data.

No analytics, tracking, or advertising services are used.
